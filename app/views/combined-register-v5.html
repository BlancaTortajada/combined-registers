What are Registers?

Registers are reliable lists of information managed by one custodian from the government organisation responsible for maintaining the Register’s data.

How do I use this Register in my service?

This register has been produced by combining data from the following Registers:

  • Local authority eng
  • Local authority sct
  • Principal local authority
  • Local authority nir
  • Local authority type

There are 2 ways to use this Register. You can either:

  • combine the Registers above using the client library included in our API documentation to automatically pull data into your service
  • download the data for this Register and keep your own copy up-to-date

What are the column headings?
country
The country's 2-letter ISO 3166-2 alpha2 code.
name
The commonly-used name of a record.
official-name
The official or technical name of a record.
citizen-names
The name of a country's citizens.
start-date
The date a record first became relevant to a register. For example, the date a country was first recognised by the UK.
end-date
The date a record stopped being applicable. For example, the date a school closed down.
