What are the column headings?
local-authority-eng
The local authority's ISO 3166-1 alpha3 code. Unique codes have been created for local authorities that don't have an existing ISO code.
local-authority-type
The type of local government organisation.
name
The commonly-used name of a record.
official-name
The official or technical name of a record.
start-date
The date a record first became relevant to a register.
end-date
The date a record stopped being applicable.
