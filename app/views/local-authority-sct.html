What are Registers?

Registers are reliable lists of information managed by one custodian from the government organisation responsible for maintaining the Registerâ€™s data.

You can easily combine data from some Registers with data from another Register to build your service. For example, this Register can be combined with other registers to create the Local authorities in the UK register.

How do I use this Register in my service?

There are 2 ways to use Registers. You can either:

  • use an application programming interface (API) to automatically pull data from a register into your service
  • download the data for this Register and keep your own copy up-to-date
